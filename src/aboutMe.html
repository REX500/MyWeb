This is me!

I was born in the capital of Croatia, Zagreb on 4th of June 1996. as a Son of Darko and Snjezana. Growing up I spent quite a lot of time in the forest thanks to two weekend houses that we had. There I learned how to ride a bike, motor bike, play in the forest, learned to respect the nature and so on. Not long after I started training sports. I always had a dedication towards sports and wanted to be the best. Search for the right sport wasn't easy but I found it in running. Started when I was 15 and not long after I joined the athletics club Dinamo Zrinjevac where I discovered that I am far more capable then I though before. That shaped me for life, put me in the mind state that I am now in. I went to a Technical school where I was thought how electronics work. We did stuff from soldering our own electric circuits to programing in C and assembler. Not to be mistaken, running wasnt the only big accomplishment, coming to Denmark and getting settled was something that I am still working on but I know that my past shaped me in a person that is able to succeed no matter what!

Active lifestyle is what defines me

Ever since I can remember I was doing sports. Since I could walk I did tennis, swimming, basketball, football, airsoft, climbing, mtb driving, motorcicyling, martial arts, athletics and now gym. I believe that us as human we are defined by moving, we simply cannot function without exercise and therefore I urge everyone I meet to get of their buts and start doing something!
